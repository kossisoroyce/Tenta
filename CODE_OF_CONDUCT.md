# Code of Conduct

This project is intended to be a respectful, serious, and collaborative space
for building decision infrastructure.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive, specific, and kind in technical feedback.
- Assume good intent, but take impact seriously.
- Discuss sensitive ML, security, risk, healthcare, financial, identity, and
  governance topics responsibly.
- Respect privacy and never request or share real sensitive operational data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing secrets, private data, sensitive operational details, or real
  decision records.
- Encouraging misuse, evasion, harmful automation, or unsafe deployment of
  decision systems.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close issues, or restrict participation when
behavior harms the safety or integrity of the project.
