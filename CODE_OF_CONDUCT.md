# Code of Conduct

This project is intended to be a respectful, serious, and collaborative space.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive, specific, and kind in technical feedback.
- Assume good intent, but take impact seriously.
- Discuss sensitive financial, fraud, and security topics responsibly.
- Respect privacy and never request or share real customer financial data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing secrets, private data, or sensitive operational details.
- Encouraging fraud, evasion, or misuse of financial systems.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior harms the safety or integrity of the project.
